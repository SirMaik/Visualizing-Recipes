Background

Recipe Analysis and Search is based on the project work in the lecture Big Data Visual Analytics in Life Sciences by Prof. Dr. Michael Krone at the Faculty of Science of Eberhard Karls University of Tübingen.

Together, we explore the data of the recipes from Food.com . This project sheds light on the many facets of diffrent cuisines and ingredients of the recipes.
